Let \(R\) be a commutative ring, \(r \in R\) an element of \(R\text{,}\) and \(S = \{r^j \mid j \geq 1\}\) the multiplicatively closed subset of \(R\) generated by \(r\text{.}\) There is an isomorphism
\begin{equation*} \alpha: R[y]/(1 -yr) \xrightarrow{\cong} S^{-1}R \end{equation*}
of \(R\)-algebras given by \(\alpha(\overline{g(y)}) =g\left(\frac{1}{r}\right).\)
