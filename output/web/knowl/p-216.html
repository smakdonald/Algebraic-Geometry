Example 1. The dimension of \(\mathbb{A}^n_k\) is clearly at least \(n\) since
\begin{equation*} \emptyset \ne \mathbb{A}^0_k \subsetneq \mathbb{A}^1_k \subsetneq \mathbb{A}^3_k \subsetneq \cdots \subsetneq \mathbb{A}^{n}_k. \end{equation*}
It is much less obvious that it is exactly \(n\text{.}\)
