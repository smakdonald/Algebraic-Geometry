Corollary 1.2.13.

If \(k\) is algebraically closed, then for any ideal \(J\) of \(k[x_1, \dots, x_n]\text{,}\) there is a bijection
\begin{equation*} V(J) \xrightarrow{\cong} \operatorname{mSpec}(k[x_1, \dots, x_n]/J) \end{equation*}
given by sending \((a_1, \dots, a_n)\) to \(\langle x_1 - a_1, \dots, x_n - a_n \rangle/J\text{.}\)
