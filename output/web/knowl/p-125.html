Assume \(k\) is an algebraically closed field and \(W \subseteq \mathbb{A}^n_k\) an affine variety. There is a bijective correspondence between affine subvarieties of \(W\) and radical ideals of \(A(W)\text{,}\) given by sending \(V \subseteq W\) to the ideal \(I(V)/I(W)\) of \(A(W)\text{.}\)
