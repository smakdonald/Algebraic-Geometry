Remark 1. Observe that every maximal chain of irreducible closed subsets (i.e., every chain that cannot be made longer and inserting new terms) of \(X\) has \(Y_n\) equal to an irreducible component of \(X\) and has \(Y_0\) equal to a single point. In particular,
\begin{equation*} \operatorname{dim}(X) = \max \{\operatorname{dim}(Y) \mid \text{$Y$ is an irreducible component of $X$}\} \end{equation*}
and
\begin{equation*} \operatorname{dim}(X) = \max \{\operatorname{codim}_X(P) \mid \text{$P$ is any point of $X$}\}. \end{equation*}
