For an algebraically closed field \(k\text{,}\) every maximal ideal of \(k[x_1, \dots, x_n]\) is of the form \((x_1 - a_1, \dots, x_n - a_n)\) for elements \(a_1, \dots, a_n \in k\text{.}\) In fact, the function
\begin{equation*} \Psi: \mathbb{A}^n_k \to \operatorname{mSpec}k[x_1, \dots, x_n] \end{equation*}
sending \((a_1 \dots, a_n)\) to \((x_1 - a_1, \dots, x_n - a_n)\) is a bijection.
