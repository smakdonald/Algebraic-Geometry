Given a closed irreducible subset \(Y\) of \(X\text{,}\) the codimension of \(Y\) in \(X\text{,}\) written \(\operatorname{codim}_X(Y)\text{,}\) the supremum over all \(n\) such that a choice of the form
\begin{equation*} Y = Y_0 \subsetneq Y_1 \subsetneq \cdots \subsetneq Y_n \subseteq X. \end{equation*}
