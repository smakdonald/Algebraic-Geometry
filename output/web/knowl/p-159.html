Definition 1. A distinguished open subset of an affine variety \(X\) is an open subset of the form
\begin{equation*} D_X(f) = \{ P \in X \mid f(P) \ne 0\} \end{equation*}
for some element \(f \in A(X)\text{.}\) (We can also think of \(D_X(f) = X \cap D(f)\) for \(f \in k[x_1, \dots, x_n]\text{.}\))
