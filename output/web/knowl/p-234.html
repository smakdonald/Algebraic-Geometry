If \(V_X(f) \cap X_i \subsetneq X_i\) for all \(1 \leq i \leq k\) (i.e., \(f\) is not identically zero on any irreducible component of \(X\) of maximal dimension) and \(V_X(f) \cap X_i \ne \emptyset\) for at least one \(i\) such that \(1 \leq i \leq k\) (i.e., \(f\) has a zero on at least one irreducible component of \(X\) of maximal dimension), then \(\operatorname{dim}V_X(f) = \operatorname{dim}(X) - 1\text{.}\)
