For any field \(k\) and integer \(n \geq 0\text{,}\) if \({\mathfrak m}\) is any maximal ideal of the polynomial ring \(k[x_1, \dots, x_n]\text{,}\) then the canonical map \(k \to k[x_1, \dots, x_n]/{\mathfrak m}\) (given as the composition of the inclusion of \(k\) into \(k[x_1, \dots, x_n]\) followed by the canonical quotient map) is a finite extension of fields.
