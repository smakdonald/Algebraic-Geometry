Proposition 1.1.21.

For any field \(k\) the following hold:
  1. Given a radical ideal \(J\) of \(k[x_1, \dots, x_n]\) we have
    \begin{equation*} I(V(J)) \supseteq J. \end{equation*}
    (This holds in fact for any subset \(J\text{.}\))
  2. Given an affine variety \(W\) in \(\mathbb{A}^n_k\) we have
    \begin{equation*} V(I(W)) = W \end{equation*}
    (For any subset W, we have \(V(I(W)) \supseteq W\))

Proof.
