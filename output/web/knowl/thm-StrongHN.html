Theorem 1.2.2.

Assume \(k\) is an algebraically closed. Then
\begin{equation*} I(V(J)) = J { \text{ and } }V(I(W)) = W \end{equation*}
for any radical ideal \(J\) of \(k[x_1, \dots, x_n]\) and any affine variety \(W\) in \(\mathbb{A}^n_k\text{.}\)
Thus, there is a order-reversing bijection between the poset (under containment) of all affine varieties in \(\mathbb{A}^n_k\) and the poset (under containment) of all radical ideals in \(k[x_1, \dots, x_n]\text{.}\)
